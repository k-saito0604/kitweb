[GGH5.X-6.X] DPIスケーリング機能をWindows
7のパソコンで使用すると，Windows 10

へのアップグレードをご検討下さい．

    It is old architecture and to fix this issue they need to upgrade to windows 10. As there is nothing we can do
    here on windows 7.
